About the company

We’re Trusted Import Export Company.

Ethiomama Investment Group began as a modest coffee exporter and has since evolved into a diversified agricultural powerhouse, playing a pivotal role in Ethiopia’s economic growth. Our journey reflects the dedication of our team, the resilience of Ethiopian farmers, and the unparalleled quality of Ethiopia’s agricultural products.

Today, we stand as a trusted partner in the agricultural sector, bridging local producers with global markets while fostering sustainable development.
